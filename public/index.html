Your Health, Connected and Secure

WellAxis DTI is a unified platform for Patients, Doctors, Hospitals, and Admins to securely manage healthcare records, appointments, prescriptions, and approvals — with consent-first sharing and audit logging.

Explore Options Patient

What You Can Do

Patients

Manage your profile, view encrypted health records, add symptoms, book appointments, and control data sharing via consent.

Doctors

Search/view patient info with consent or emergency override, create prescriptions, and review reports.

Hospitals

Upload lab reports to patient records, manage hospital profile, and collaborate across care teams.

Admins

Approve hospitals and doctors, oversee role assignments, and monitor audit logs for compliance.
